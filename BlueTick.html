  • Who qualifies for and how can I earn the Checkmark or Blue Tick ?


    The blue tick, or Verified Badge, is for those who contribute to IT Off-Topics and class representatives (CRs). If you're into tech discussions or help out with IT-related things here, or if you're the class rep in your class, you're eligible for the blue tick as a recognition for your contributions.

    To apply for the blue tick, follow these steps:

    1. Click on 'Apply for Blue Tick' to begin the process.

    2. A Google Form will open—complete all the required fields.

    3. Upload necessary photos as specified in the form.

    4. Submit your form.

    5. Wait for an email confirmation within 24 hours. If approved, you'll proceed to the next step.

    6. Once you receive the confirmation email, the checkmark will be displayed next to your name within 24-48 hours.


    Note: The blue tick serves as a mark of distinction and trust within our IT community. It signifies active participation and credibility in IT discussions and activities. We encourage all eligible members to apply and showcase their dedication to the field of IT Off-Topics.
